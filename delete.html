Data Deletion Request

How to Request Deletion

To request the permanent deletion of your submitted feedback data, please send an email to our support address: support@nishanthkesavan.in.

Since our platform is anonymous, we have no way of knowing your identity. To locate the specific feedback you submitted, you must include the following verifiable details in your email:

  • The 10-digit PNR Number of the journey.
  • The 5-digit Train Number of the journey.
  • The exact Date of Journey (in dd-mm-yyyy format).

Once we receive your request and verify the details, the corresponding feedback report will be permanently deleted from our database.
