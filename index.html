RailHygiene

Cleaner Train Journeys, Powered by You.

Ever wondered about the cleanliness of your train coach before you board? RailHygiene is a community-driven app that allows passengers to anonymously rate and review the hygiene of train coaches across Indian Railways, creating a public database of cleanliness ratings. Check past reviews, contribute your own feedback, and help make train travel better for everyone.
